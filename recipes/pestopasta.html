Pesto Pasta

Recipes

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is thhe perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese
