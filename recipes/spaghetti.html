Basic Spaghetti

Recipe's

Basic spaghetti only contains a small portion of ingredients

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1(16ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup shredded mild Cheddar chheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook beef in a large skillet over medium-high hit
  3. Meanwhile, bring alrge pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Stir in spaghetti; cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
  4. Add spaghetti to meat mixture; mix well. Transfer to a 9x13-inch dish. Top with Cheddar cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheese is bubbly, about 30 minutes.
